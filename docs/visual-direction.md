# Visual Direction — AquaReport Demo Wizard
## Source: Founder, May 2025

## Core Philosophy
The interface should communicate "premium guided experience" NOT "advanced SaaS dashboard."

The homeowner should feel:
- They're being guided through something valuable
- The company is sophisticated  
- The presentation is calm and confident
- The information is curated intentionally

NOT:
- They're looking at software
- They're reading analytics
- They're being overloaded with information

"One powerful idea per screen" = the visual philosophy.

## 1. Typography System (4 levels only)

| Level | Size | Use |
|-------|------|-----|
| Hero | 48–64px | Water score, score transformation, pricing, major emotional moments ONLY |
| Section title | 28–36px | "Your Home Water Profile", "Top Water Concerns", "Your Home Water Plan" |
| Body | 16–18px | High readability, taller line-height, less text overall |
| Caption | 12–14px | Muted, supportive only |

No tiny dense labels. Big typography + whitespace = perceived confidence.

## 2. Color Palette (5 colors + 3 semantic)

| Role | Value |
|------|-------|
| Background | #070B14 |
| Surface | #101826 |
| Elevated surface | #162033 |
| Primary accent | #6FD3FF |
| Primary glow | rgba(111, 211, 255, 0.18) |
| Success | #6EE7B7 |
| Warning | #FBBF24 |
| Critical | #FB7185 (only for actual danger moments) |

Use color for: emphasis, active states, score progression, important moments.
NOT decoration.

## 3. Card System
- Kill glass-on-glass (81 instances of bg-white/[0.03])
- Reduce border opacity dramatically
- Many cards → background + spacing only
- Whitespace creates separation, not outlines
- Cards = quiet containers, not floating widgets

## 4. Score Gauge (signature visual)
- Apple Health ring feeling
- Thicker arc, softer edge gradients, subtle bloom/glow
- Animated number count-up with spring easing
- Delayed reveal timing
- Tiny ambient pulse after completion
- Center number dominates visually
- Score Journey: ONE dominant gauge morphing between states (30→55→94), NOT 3 side-by-side

## 5. Transition Overlays
- Keynote chapter transitions, not modals
- Darker backdrop, huge centered text, almost no UI chrome
- Subtle blur, slower fade timing, staggered text appearance

## 6. Customer Concerns
- Apple onboarding selection cards
- Larger cards, more spacing, fewer words
- Icon + short emotional phrase
- Tactile press feedback
- Selected state should feel satisfying

## 7. Pricing
- Huge monthly payment as primary focus
- Simplified hierarchy: monthly → total investment → includes list
- Calm, transparent, premium. NOT salesy.
- No stacked promo feeling

## 8. Summary Screen
- Premium recommendation / personalized plan feeling
- Lots of whitespace, simplified sections
- Score journey large, concerns summarized, recommendations clean
- Fewer cards, more vertical rhythm

## 9. Motion
- Emotional moments = slower (score reveal, journey, pricing, summary) — spring easing
- Utility moments = faster (navigation, tabs, dropdowns)
- Staggered reveal timing
- Smoother continuity between steps

## 10. Anti-pattern: "Component Visible"
The homeowner should NEVER mentally think "here are 5 widgets."
They should think "I'm moving through one guided experience."
- Fewer boundaries, boxes, separations
- Stronger hierarchy, cleaner focus
- Holistic feeling, not component-based
